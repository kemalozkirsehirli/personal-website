About

With fortitude,



   I transition phases,


      I metamorphose


      complexities and abstractions


      into


      parsable, representable, computable


      structures,



         I force these


         compiled, disentangled, transmuted


         systems


         to


         surrender, confess, elucidate


         their governing rules,



                                          I, only then, execute:



Kemal.
