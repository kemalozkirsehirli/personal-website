My work and the Özkırşehirli Group aim to make difficult scientific systems parsable, computable, verifiable, and decision-ready. (1) We develop MeshAnyOrder, an order-agnostic autoregressive transformer for topology-aware 3D mesh generation from point clouds. (2) We build multi-stage CADD pipelines for TBXT/brachyury, integrating docking, QSAR, CNN rescoring, co-folding, free-energy scaffolding, selectivity, and sourceability. (3) Scientific-software directions include VeriQSM + QSMBench for artifact-grounded verification of scientific false success, Kadanoff-GNN-RG for learned coarse-graining and phase analysis, Kupcinet–Getz Reaction–Diffusion AI for solver-faithful benchmarking, DFT → kMC for auditable multiscale kinetics, and a structure-informed antibody-RL scaffold. (4) I develop and evaluate generative, foundation, and agentic systems for protein design, chromatin organization, autoimmune target discovery, molecular simulation, and robust scientific decision-making.

AI/ML for Science - Selected Work

Molecular Simulation, CADD, and Chemical Physics

TBXT - 11-person chordoma/TBXT hit-identification project targeting PDB 6F59 site F; 2,274 prior-art compounds, 737 analogs, 30,000 BRICS recombinations, a 570-compound novelty-filtered pool, and a docking/QSAR/GNINA/Boltz-2/MMGBSA-FEP/selectivity funnel

VeriQSM + QSMBench - verification-first scientific-agent framework for quantum chemistry and statistical mechanics: typed workflow IR, static and physics verifiers, bounded repair/refusal policies, provenance ledgers, PySCF-compatible calculations, trajectory/stat-mech analyses, and explicit separation of nominal completion from scientifically verified success

Kadanoff-GNN-RG - hierarchical-lattice renormalization-group framework for random-bond spin-s Ising models; direct continuum RG operators, finite-spin distribution comparison, symmetry-aware features, Wasserstein/MMD-style distances, and uncertainty-aware phase-topology analysis

Buchwald Lab - CuH-catalyst development for asymmetric methylation and alkylation of vinyl boronate esters, with 1H-NMR, chiral-HPLC, substrate-scope expansion, and multi-gram scale-up

Pedal AI - Python/LangGraph and FastAPI infrastructure for chemistry agents, molecular-prediction engines, inspectable retrosynthesis trees, provenance, and decision-ready synthesis workflows

Kupcinet–Getz Reaction–Diffusion AI - reaction–diffusion scientific-computing framework for nonlinear oscillators, traveling waves, and synthetic morphogenesis; deterministic ODE solvers, chemical-Langevin dynamics, Gillespie SSA, spatial RDME, convergence audits, calibration, and abstention

Sepal AI / Mercor - Graduate-level chemistry LLM data, retrosynthesis and reaction-mechanism tasks, chemical-validity rubrics, prompt analysis, and failure-mode annotation

DFT → kMC - mechanism-aware decision-stability pipeline linking Gaussian/ORCA thermochemistry to Eyring rates, ODE/CME/Gillespie kinetics, solvent/pathway/product ranking, uncertainty checks, and provenance-rich scientific reports

Biomolecular AI, Protein, and Genome Modeling
